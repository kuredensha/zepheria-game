    About

    Zepheria is a 2D browser-based MMORPG adventure inspired by BrowserQuest. It offers a unique gaming experience, drawing from community-driven forks. Our goal is to provide gamers with a timeless multiplayer experience while preserving the nostalgic appeal of the original concept. Zepheria is dedicated to delivering immersive gameplay to players worldwide. We've preserved and expanded upon the game's original assets to maintain its nostalgic appeal. Zepheria is a rare community-driven project committed to delivering immersive gameplay worldwide.
